## Annual Occasions (Gregorian-fixed)

### Saudi National Day — September 23
- **High-eng rate:** 75% (n=12)
- **Lead time:** 21 days
- **Format:** Carousel or Video
- **Composition:** Digital graphic + architectural framing
- **Lighting:** Dramatic moody or flat graphic
- **Pattern:** `occasion_specific_greeting` + `heritage_storytelling_hook`
- **Caption:** Long Arabic, patriotic register, heritage references
- **Avoid:** Generic flag graphics with no brand soul

### Saudi Founding Day — February 22
- **High-eng rate:** 86% (n=7)
- **Lead time:** 14 days
- **Format:** Video or Digital graphic
- **Composition:** Graphic text + product hero
- **Lighting:** Dramatic moody
- **Pattern:** `cultural_object_hero` + `heritage_storytelling_hook`
- **Caption:** Formal Arabic, historical framing, connect brand to Saudi story
- **Avoid:** Copy-paste national day content — founding day has a distinct heritage energy

## Hijri Occasions (date varies annually)

### Ramadan
- **High-eng rate:** 62% (n=26)
- **Lead time:** 21 days
- **Format:** Image or Carousel
- **Composition:** Overhead spread or architectural framing
- **Lighting:** Warm practical or dramatic moody
- **Pattern:** `occasion_specific_greeting` + `family_majlis_hierarchical` + `heritage_storytelling_hook`
- **Caption:** Long Arabic, reverent tone, Ramadan-specific vocabulary
- **3-phase structure:**
  - Phase 1 (days 1–10): Welcome/greeting content
  - Phase 2 (days 11–20): Heritage/tradition content
  - Phase 3 (last 10 days): Laylat Al-Qadr focus, reflective tone
- **Avoid:** Aggressive commercial promos during prayer times; food imagery during fasting hours

### Eid Al-Fitr
- **High-eng rate:** 78% (n=9)
- **Lead time:** 21 days
- **Format:** Image (branded occasion card) or Carousel
- **Composition:** Studio clean or overhead spread
- **Lighting:** Cold studio (for cards) or warm practical (for gatherings)
- **Pattern:** `occasion_specific_greeting` + `limited_time_promo` (if applicable)
- **Caption:** Warm celebratory Arabic, Eid greeting, brief
- **Avoid:** Heavy discount messaging — Eid is emotional, not transactional

### Eid Al-Adha ⚠️
- **High-eng rate:** 11% (n=9) — LOWEST of all occasions
- **Lead time:** 7 days (reduce investment)
- **Note:** Corpus shows this is consistently the worst-performing occasion.
  Reduce content volume; shift budget to Eid Al-Fitr or evergreen.
- **If you must post:** Keep it simple — greeting card only, no product push

### Hajj Season
- **High-eng rate:** 60% (n=5)
- **Lead time:** 14 days
- **Tone:** Respectful, understated — not commercial
- **Pattern:** `cultural_object_hero` or `community_pride_statement`
- **Avoid:** Product promotion; treat this as brand-values content only

## Campaign Seasons

### Winter Seasonal (Dec–Jan)
- **High-eng rate:** 100% (n=4)
- **Highest-performing season in corpus**
- **Format:** Carousel
- **Setting:** Outdoor nature or cosy indoor
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight or warm practical
- **Pattern:** Any — this season amplifies all content
- **Action:** Double content investment in Dec–Jan

### Summer Campaign (Jun–Aug)
- **High-eng rate:** 78% (n=9)
- **Format:** Image
- **Setting:** Outdoor nature
- **Lighting:** Natural daylight
- **Pattern:** `lifestyle_integrated` (exception — works in summer despite low avg)
- **Tone:** Fresh, energetic, lighter register

## Evergreen Content Strategy

**Baseline:** 47% high-engagement (solid)
**Target:** Keep evergreen at 70–80% of output, seasonal at 20–30%
**Current corpus split:** 84% evergreen — opportunity to shift 14pp more to seasonal

### Evergreen content that beats baseline

- `heritage_storytelling_hook` — 100% high-eng (works any time)
- `overhead_spread` composition — 64% high-eng
- Arabic-only long captions — 51% high-eng
- Carousel format — 74% high-eng
- Sunday publish — 61% high-eng
