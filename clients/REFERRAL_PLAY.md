# THE REFERRAL-ASK PLAY (B150, June 12)
*The cheapest client acquisition is a happy client's voice. The play is timed, never begged.*

## WHEN the ask fires (any ONE of these — never before)
- Client taps ✅ انشر on a full batch with ZERO edits (the trust ladder's strongest signal)
- Client sends unprompted praise (voice note, «ما شاء الله», a screenshot share)
- Trust reaches L1 (10 untouched approvals — the system's own milestone)

## THE ASK (WhatsApp, Stage-0, one message — قالب جاهز)
«يسعدنا إن المحتوى عاجبك 🌟 لو عندك صاحب مشروع تحس محتواه يستاهل نفس الشغل —
عرّفنا عليه وأول أسبوع محتوى له هدية منك.»

## THE MECHANICS
- New client arrives via referral → intake records `referred_by: <handle>` in manifest.json
- The referrer's reward: one week of content credited (cost to us ≈ 7 cards ≈ $0.25 — money law smiles)
- Referral chains are READ from manifests (`grep referred_by clients/*/manifest.json`) — no separate ledger
- The ask counts as a PING in the trust meter (B103) — budget applies; never ask twice in 60 days

## NEVER
- Ask after an edit-heavy batch or any rejection (read the room — the ledger IS the room)
- Ask a throttled/dormant client (myfitness-class) — revival comes first
- Automate the send (Stage-0 law: a human sends it; the machine only stages the moment)
