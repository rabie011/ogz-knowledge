## The principle
His **taste is the moat**; his **attention is the scarcest resource**; he **judges results,
not drafts** (Rule #9). The gate map resolves the tension stage by stage — a tap where
taste must enter, autonomy where a standing law already carries his taste. Taps must
**fall over the 90 days** as gold accumulates (PHASE2's whole thesis).

## The graduation rule (the 90-day mechanism)
ONE ratchet, lifted from trust.json, **per-client AND per-occasion-family**:
**FULL → SAMPLED → AUTONOMOUS climbs ONLY by COUNTED clean batches** — never by time,
never AI-advanced. 3 clean idea/brief batches drop a family one notch; 6 clean (rejection
≤ half baseline, sustained 2 batches — the PHASE2 pass curve) settles it. **ASYMMETRIC:**
relax earned slowly; escalate INSTANT on any single trigger (new client, major occasion
never relaxes below SAMPLED, one culture_breach, rising rejection, a newly-minted law).
One edit/reject resets that family's counter to 0. The LAW floor never relaxes —
graduation removes only HIS tap. gold accumulates → pens imitate → rejection falls →
taps fall. That IS the test.
